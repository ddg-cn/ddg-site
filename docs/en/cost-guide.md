# Due Diligence Cost Guide

Supply chain Due Diligence (DD) costs vary significantly based on region, industry, company size, audit standards, and compliance maturity. This guide provides actionable cost benchmarks for two core audiences:

- **Buyers / Auditing Clients** (Brands, Retailers, Traders): Total expenditures for operating DD programs, including audit fees, platform fees, personnel costs, and non-compliance risks.
- **Auditees / Suppliers** (Factories, Manufacturers, Service Providers): Initial investment for building an internal compliance system from scratch and ongoing operational costs.

All amounts are primarily in **USD**, with **CNY** references in parentheses (refer to central bank or major bank exchange rates). Data provided is for budget planning references only; specific projects should be based on actual supplier quotes.

---

## Part 1: Cost Structure for Buyers / Auditing Clients

### 1.1 Third-Party Audit Daily Rates

Audit agencies price most projects by **Auditor Day**. Understanding daily rates helps buyers maintain bargaining power when selecting agencies or negotiating framework agreements.

| Agency Type | Mainland China | SE Asia (Vietnam/Indonesia/Bangladesh) | South Asia (India/Pakistan) | Latin America (Brazil/Mexico) | Eastern Europe |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 International** (SGS, BV, Intertek, TÜV) | $500–900 / day | $550–1,000 / day | $500–950 / day | $650–1,200 / day | $600–1,100 / day |
| **Tier 2 / Specialized** (Elevate, QIMA, Arche) | $450–750 / day | $450–850 / day | $400–800 / day | $600–1,000 / day | $550–950 / day |
| **Local Accredited** (CNCA Accredited in China) | $300–600 / day | $350–650 / day | $300–600 / day | $400–800 / day | $400–750 / day |

## Part 2: Compliance Establishment and Operational Costs for Auditees / Suppliers

For suppliers, compliance is not just an audit fee; it is a long-term operational investment.

### 2.1 Initial Investment (Fixed Costs)

| Item | Description | Estimated Cost (USD) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Facility Upgrades** | Fire safety (alarms, exits), PPE, ventilation, waste storage, machine guarding | $2,000–20,000+ (Highly variable) |
| **Management System** | Drafting SOPs, internal manuals, hiring compliance officers | $3,000–8,000 |
| **Software/Hardware** | Access control, attendance systems, environmental monitoring sensors | $1,500–5,000 |
| **Consulting/Training** | Pre-audit counseling, internal auditor training | $2,000–6,000 |

### 2.2 Ongoing Operational Costs (Annual)

| Item | Description | Estimated Cost (USD) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Audit/Certification Fees** | Annual renewal, third-party platform fees (Sedex/amfori/EcoVadis) | $1,500–4,000 |
| **Labor Compliance** | Overtime premiums, social insurance, benefits (Key cost driver) | 5%–15% of total payroll |
| **EHS Maintenance** | Periodic environmental testing (noise, air, water), waste disposal fees | $2,000–7,000 |
| **Personnel Training** | Ongoing safety and ethics training for all staff | $1,000–3,000 |

## Core Variables Affecting Costs

| Variable | Impact | Explanation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Current Baseline** | ±30–60% | The larger the gap, the higher the rectification cost. A Gap Assessment is a necessary pre-budget investment. |
| **Factory Location** | ±20–40% | Costs in South/East China are slightly higher than North China. |
| **Agency Choice** | ±20–40% | International Tier 1 vs. Local; confirm buyer acceptance beforehand. |
| **Consulting Support** | +20–35% | Consultants improve pass rates but add to total upfront costs. |
| **Integrated Audits** | –20–35% | Combining multiple standards into one audit saves significant auditor days. |
| **Staff Size** | Linear | Agencies price based on headcount tiers; larger factories pay more. |

***

**Three-Year Average Annualized Cost:** For a typical 200-person factory, the annualized cost to maintain a "High Compliance" status (Tier 1 Buyer requirements) is approximately **$15,000–$25,000** (excluding labor/benefit costs).

:::warning Important Note
All data provided is a **benchmark baseline** based on industry practices from Sedex, amfori, SAI, BSR, ILO, and various certification bodies as of **2025**. Actual quotes depend on real-time market conditions.
:::
